# Copiedcatz - 2 Minute Demo Script

---

## HOOK (15 sec)
> "Ever tried to recreate a photo's vibe with AI? You type 'dramatic lighting, shallow depth of field'... and get something completely wrong. That's because prompts are vague. There's no standard format—until now."

---

## SOLUTION (15 sec)
> "Copiedcatz extracts Visual DNA from any image. Upload a photo, get a structured JSON recipe with camera angles, lighting, composition—everything. Copy it, share it, remix it."

---

## LIVE DEMO (60 sec)

### Upload (10 sec)
*Drag image to dashboard*
> "I'll upload this product shot. Watch the extraction..."

### Show JSON (20 sec)
*Click Code icon when editor loads*
> "Here's the Visual DNA—structured JSON. Camera angle: low. Lighting: studio softbox, 45 degrees. Depth of field: shallow f/2.8. Every visual parameter, captured."

### Copy (10 sec)
*Click Copy JSON*
> "One click to copy. Share with your team, use in other tools, or save as a template."

### Edit & Generate (20 sec)
*Type in Quick Edit: "Make it more dramatic"*
> "Want changes? Just type. 'Make it more dramatic.' Gemini updates only what you asked."

*Click Generate Variation*
> "Hit generate—same DNA, new image. Consistent every time."

---

## CLOSE (30 sec)
> "Copiedcatz turns any image into a reusable recipe. No more prompt roulette.

> Built with Bria's FIBO for extraction, Gemini for JSON conversion, and a no-code editor for remixing.

> Extract once. Remix forever. That's Copiedcatz."

---

## Total: ~2 minutes

### Key phrases to emphasize:
- **"Visual DNA"** - your differentiator
- **"Structured JSON"** - technical credibility
- **"Copy, share, remix"** - the value prop
- **"Consistent every time"** - the outcome
